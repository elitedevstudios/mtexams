# Jamaica NSC Grade 2 Curriculum Extract
Source: MOE-NSC-GRADE-2-Int.-Studies-Language-Math-FINAL.pdf

## GRADE 2 THEMES

## LANGUAGE ARTS STRANDS
### Word Recognition & Fluency (Phonics)
- Sequence words in alphabetical order
- Identify initial/medial/final letter sounds
- Differentiate CVC and CVCe patterns (short/long vowels)
- Decode words with consonant digraphs (sh, ch, th, wh)
- Decode words with consonant blends (bl, cl, st, tr, etc.)
- Identify vowel diphthongs (ow, ou, oi, oy)
- Distinguish voiced/voiceless th digraph
- Spell rhyming words using phonics
- Use onset and rimes to spell unfamiliar words

### Grammar & Conventions
- Personal pronouns (I/me, you, he/him, she/her, it, we, they)
- Possessive pronouns (ours, yours, theirs)
- Singular/plural nouns (-s, -es, irregular)
- Proper nouns with capitals
- Subject-verb agreement
- Conjunctions (and)
- Past tense of 'to be'

### Reading Comprehension
- Make predictions about text
- Sequence events
- Interpret information
- Identify main idea and details
- Use context clues

### Writing
- Write complete sentences
- Support content with facts and details
- Use the writing process
- Write informational and narrative pieces

## MATHEMATICS STRANDS
### Number (0-999)
- Compare sets (more, less, same)
- Count objects up to 999
- Group objects into tens and hundreds
- Read/write numerals and number words to 999
- Place value: hundreds, tens, ones
- Expanded notation
- Compare and order numbers
- Count by 2s, 5s, 10s

### Operations
- Addition facts to 20 with fluency
- Subtraction facts to 20 with fluency
- Add/subtract 2-digit numbers
- Word problems with addition/subtraction
- Introduction to multiplication concepts

### Measurement
- Tell time to hour and half-hour
- Jamaican currency (coins and notes)
- Length, mass, capacity comparisons
- Days of week, months of year

### Geometry
- 2D shapes: circle, triangle, square, rectangle
- 3D shapes: sphere, cube, cone, cylinder
- Sides and corners
- Symmetry

## INTEGRATED STUDIES THEMES
### Term 1: Who Am I? / My Body
- Brain, heart, skeleton functions
- Keeping the body healthy
- The five senses
- Personal hygiene
- Exercise and nutrition

### Civics & Social Studies
- National symbols of Jamaica
- National heroes
- Community helpers
- Jamaica's parishes
- Rights and responsibilities

### Science
- Living and non-living things
- Plants and animals
- Weather and seasons
- Water cycle basics
- Materials and properties

### HFLE (Health & Family Life)
- Healthy eating habits
- Personal safety
- Hygiene practices
- Expressing feelings
